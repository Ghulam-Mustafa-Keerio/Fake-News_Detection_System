üß† How It Works

1. Text Preprocessing: The system cleans and normalizes input text by removing URLs, special characters, and stopwords, then applies stemming.

2. Feature Extraction: Using TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency), the text is converted into numerical features that capture the importance of words in the context of the entire corpus.

3. Classification: A Passive Aggressive Classifier, trained on labeled data, analyzes the features and predicts whether the text is fake or real news.

4. Confidence Score: The system provides a confidence level for its prediction, helping users understand the certainty of the classification.
